## Overview

The Flutter Billing App is a high-performance, **offline-first** Point of Sale (POS) and billing application built with Flutter. Designed for seamless retail checkout operations, it features barcode scanning, thermal Bluetooth printing, and robust local data persistence—all without requiring an active internet connection.

## Key Capabilities

### Product Management System
Complete CRUD operations for inventory items with integrated barcode/QR code support. Add, edit, and delete products from your local catalog with ease.

### Smart Checkout System
Rapid cart building via camera-based barcode scanning or manual entry. Real-time order calculation with automatic total computation.

### Bluetooth Thermal Printing
Direct integration with thermal printers using the `print_bluetooth_thermal` package. Instantly output physical receipts with customizable shop details.

### Shop Settings & Customization
Centrally managed shop configuration that dynamically appears on printed receipts—including shop name, address, contact details, and tax information.

### Offline-First Architecture
Powered by Hive for lightning-fast NoSQL local data storage. No active internet connectivity required for any core operations.

## Technical Highlights

<Info>
Built using **Clean Architecture** and **Feature-Driven Design** principles, ensuring scalability, separation of concerns, and robust testability.
</Info>

**Core Technologies:**
- **Framework**: Flutter (SDK >=3.1.0)
- **State Management**: `flutter_bloc` with `equatable`
- **Dependency Injection**: `get_it`
- **Navigation**: `go_router`
- **Local Database**: `hive` & `hive_flutter`
- **Functional Programming**: `fpdart` for Either-based error handling
- **Hardware Integration**: `mobile_scanner`, `print_bluetooth_thermal`

## Use Cases in Action

### Rapid Billing Entry
A cashier launches the app, navigates to the home page, and uses the device camera to instantly scan product barcodes. Products are automatically added to the cart, totals are calculated, and a receipt is finalized within seconds.

### Physical Receipt Generation
After checkout confirmation, the app triggers a connected Bluetooth thermal POS printer to instantly print an itemized paper receipt complete with the shop's header information.

### Inventory Management
A store manager opens the Product feature to add new stock to the local database, scanning the product barcode to bind the SKU for future lightning-fast checkouts.

### No-Connection Operation
The business operates at a trade show or outdoor market with unreliable networking. The app functions entirely via its embedded Hive database and Bluetooth printer—completely undisturbed by network connectivity issues.
